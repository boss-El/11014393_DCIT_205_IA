About Lecturer

Michael Agbo Tettey
Lecturer, Software Engineering
University of Ghana

Welcome to the profile of Michael Agbo Tettey, a seasoned lecturer in Software Engineering at the University of Ghana. With a profound background in software engineering and algorithm design, Michael is a critical thinker and solutions engineer, showcasing his expertise in solving complex problems with a strong mathematical foundation.

About Me

A passionate individual driven by the beauty and elegance of mathematics, I specialize in Graph Theory, Analytic Number Theory, Image Segmentation, Algorithms, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and more. My interests extend to various technologies, including Java, NoSQL, Web Services, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Databases, Financial Markets, Neo4j, Cyber Security, and Recommender Systems.

Professional Experience

I have built and led teams of software engineers, spearheading the design, implementation, and deployment of bespoke software solutions across diverse industries. As a lecturer at the University of Ghana, I am committed to imparting knowledge and fostering a deep understanding of software engineering principles among my students.

Skills

  • TypeScript
  • Engineering Management
  • Microservices
  • React.js
  • GraphQL

Notable Projects

Public Investment Management (PIM) Systems Audit (Government of Ghana):

As part of the QII and GEMS-TA support project, I audited Public Investment Management systems for the Government of Ghana through the Ministry of Finance. This involved analyzing the current PIM ecosystem, identifying bottlenecks, suggesting interim fixes, and providing recommendations to architect an optimal Public Investment Management ecosystem in the Republic of Ghana

CMS Implementation (Electricity Company of Ghana)

Worked with Indra Sistemas as a senior technical consultant on the implementation of CMS, a utility management system for the Electricity Company of Ghana. Responsibilities included client interfacing, Oracle Database Administration, RHEL Systems Administration, support and training, data analytics, and technical support.

In-House Solutions

I am responsible for the design and implementation of in-house custom software solutions and their integration with existing systems. These solutions are mostly database-driven web-based applications with web service interfaces, ensuring seamless integrations.
